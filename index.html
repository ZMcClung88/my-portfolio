About

My Programming Journey.

  • 2013

    Moved to Utah

    Seeking new opprotunities and adventure; my wife, dog and I packed up and headed to Utah. I love the outdoors and knew SLC would be a great place for a new beginning.

  • Summer 2014

    Introduced to Codecademy

    Until now, most of my career was spent in the sales industry. I was slowly losing interest in sales becasue I did not feel like I was really doing something or contributing to something bigger. A friend, who is a web developer, introduced me to Codecademy. This is when my passion for programming began!

  • December 2016

    Left The Sales World

    I officially left the sales world to start a business and to take time to teach myself programming.

  • June 2017

    Attended DevMountain

    After completing codecademy and wandering aimlessly in web developing, I decided to attend DevMountain, a web development bootcamp, to catapult my knowledge to the next level so I can produce awesome websites and contribute value to any team I join.
